about us

Pronut B.V. was founded in 2004. We like to introduce ourselves as suppliers for groundnuts and dried fruit for the human consumption and the bird feed industry. With over more than 30 years in the field of peanuts, raisins and various other type of nuts we are supply most of the European snack manufactures and bird feed industry.
